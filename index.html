Why Shakira?

Why is Shakira the best?

It’s unusual for an artist to be so talented internationally. Shakira is a globally famous, acclaimed vocalist while also being an incredible dancer, and performer. Her concerts are well-known for their incredible spectacle - the sets, the choreography - and she sells out at all events internationally. Shakira has received several awards from being Top in Billboards to music videos, and grammy nominations.

She has sold over 95 million records, making her the best-selling female latin artist of all time, and received twelve latin grammy awards. You can call her the Queen of Latin Music!
